GoodSearch

When Dan was introduced to the concept of GoodSearch, he was told that the service itself was actually terrible, but it raised money for Springfield so people were encouraged to use it as much as possible. He saw that people would search for something in GoodSearch and then search the same thing in Google to find better results. He then decided to automate that process and the Springfield Search extension was born!

THON Countdown

A few weeks before THON 2017, Dan was bored. However, he was also excited that THON was appraoching. But exactly how far away was THON? What started out as simple boredom was transformed from the hours of 12am to 3am one fateful night into the THON Countdown extension.
